Sodium

Fun Facts About: Sodium

  • Its atomic number is 11, and its atomic mass is 22.990
  • It is a part of the alkali metals, and it has a silver-like color
  • It is highly reactive on its own, and can ignite while in water
  • When bonded to chlorine, the compound NaCl is formed, which is otherwise known as salt!
  • It can be used to preserve food and cool things down
  • Sodium is the sixth most abundant element on Earth, and makes up about 2.6% of its crust
  • It plays an important role in energy production within the human body
