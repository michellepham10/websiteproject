Sodium

Fun Facts About: Sodium

  • Its atomic number is 6, and its atomic mass is 12.011 amu
  • It is a nonmetal and typically forms covalent bonds
  • Because it has 4 valence electrons, it can easily bond with many different elements
  • Carbon is known as the building block of life
  • Both graphite and diamond are made from pure carbon!
  • It plays an important role in the processes of photosynthesis and cellular respiration
  • Trapping carbon dioxide in the atmosphere causes global warming
